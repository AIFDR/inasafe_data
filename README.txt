This is a collection of spatial data supporting testing and demonstration
of InaSAFE (www.inasafe.org) which is a Free and Open Source tool designed
to estimate impacts of natural hazard to people and infrastructure.

This data is automatically downloaded as part of InaSAFE developement
installation procedure, but can also be obtained directly from
http://www.aifdr.org/svn/inasafe_data
